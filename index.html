02 Who's behind it

Own your music. Get paid for it. That's the whole deal.

25 years making records with Frank Ocean, Lorde, Foster the People, and more.

Moon Machine exists because Taylor Johnson and Phil Danyew believed independent artists deserved the same infrastructure labels have always used — without signing anything away to get it.

We distribute your music to every store and collect every dollar it earns: streaming royalties, publishing, performance rights, sync. Everything a label would go collect. We just never ask to own your music in return.
